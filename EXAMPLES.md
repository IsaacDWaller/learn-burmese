## Supabase Auth

### [Retrieve a user](https://supabase.com/docs/reference/javascript/auth-getuser)

Use `getSession().session.user` on the client.
Use `getUser()` on the server.

### [Prototyping your schema](https://www.prisma.io/docs/orm/prisma-migrate/workflows/prototyping-your-schema)

Use `npx prisma db push` to synchronise the Prisma schema with the database schema.
